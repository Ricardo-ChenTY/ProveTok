\section{Notes on Evidence Profiles}
We use the gold-mask real profile for primary claims, and we treat cross-dataset silver-label profiles as stress tests.
This separation is necessary to avoid over-claiming based on automatically generated masks (e.g., TotalSegmentator~\citep{wasserthal2023totalsegmentator}) or pseudo masks.
